Flagship Initiative

Jaza Miti: Tokenized Tree Planting

Jaza Miti goes beyond traditional tree planting by creating measurable, verifiable, and monetizable environmental impact. We solve the lack of transparency in traditional reforestation by assigning local youth as guardians and geo-tagging every sapling.

  • Data Capture

    We log GPS coordinates, species types, growth metrics, and survival rates into structured datasets.

  • Tokenization

    Each living tree is minted as a Conservation NFT on Hedera, serving as a transparent digital asset representing real-world ecological impact.

Regenerative Ecosystem Strategies

Beekeeping Initiative

Integrated with reforestation to create a self-reinforcing ecological system. "Helping bees help us."

  • Ecosystem: Enhances pollination and forest regeneration.
  • Economy: Youth harvest honey and beeswax.
  • Blockchain: Tokenizing honey batches to trace origins back to the specific forest zones.

Arts in Nature

Transforming conservation into a cultural experience. Artists become storytellers of the ecosystem.

  • Creative: Forest murals and sculpture installations.
  • Data-Linked: Art connected to specific tree clusters.
  • Blockchain: Minting Art NFTs embedded with real environmental metrics.
Trust Infrastructure

Hedera Guardian Model

Conservation has historically suffered from poor data verification and lack of trust. We use Hedera Guardian as a policy-driven, verifiable data system to solve this.

  • MRV System

    Measurement, Reporting, and Verification that is standardized, auditable, and completely transparent.

  • Policy Driven

    Automated rules for tree validation, data integrity, and ESG compliance ensure that every minted asset has real-world backing.

The Strategic Advantage

By building on an immutable ledger, we provide absolute investor trust, create a highly scalable model, and align completely with global ESG frameworks.
