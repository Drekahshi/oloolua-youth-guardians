About Oloolua Forest & Our Mission

Oloolua Forest, located in Kenya, is a vital ecosystem we're dedicated to protecting through reforestation, bamboo projects, and community involvement. As the Oloolua Youth Nursery, we focus on planting trees, empowering youth and women, and aligning with national initiatives like Jaza Miti to combat climate change.

Our technology stack, built on Hedera's distributed ledger technology (DLT), turns conservation actions into verifiable, rewarding assets. This guide explains how we use tools like Conservation NFTs, Hedera Guardian, Dovu, Evercity's DLT Earth, and RWA tokenization in our DApp to drive impact.

Our Vision: Transforming conservation into a sustainable, prosperous endeavor through blockchain technology and community engagement.

Our Core Technology Components

We leverage a comprehensive blockchain ecosystem to create transparency, trust, and value in conservation efforts:

  • Hedera Blockchain: Fast, energy-efficient, carbon-negative foundation
  • Conservation NFTs: Digital certificates representing real conservation assets
  • Hedera Guardian: Transparent verification of environmental impact
  • Dovu Protocol: Standardized carbon offset tokenization
  • Evercity DLT Earth: Scaling sustainable finance mechanisms
  • RWA Tokenization: Fractional ownership of real-world conservation assets

Carbon Credit Tokenization

Using Dovu's protocol, we tokenize carbon offsets from our reforestation projects, creating tradable assets that support our conservation work.

Example: A hectare of bamboo can generate approximately 20 carbon credits annually, each representing 1 ton of CO₂ removed from the atmosphere.

Conservation NFT Marketplace

Hedera: Our Blockchain Foundation

Hedera is our core platform—a fast, energy-efficient, carbon-negative blockchain ideal for eco-projects. It provides low-cost transactions and strong security, aligning with our ESG goals.

In our undertakings: We deploy our DApp on Hedera to mint tokens via Proof of Growth (PoG), where verified plantings trigger rewards. This creates a secure ledger for tracking everything from seedlings to carbon offsets.

Key Advantage: Hedera's carbon-negative footprint means our technology infrastructure actually contributes to environmental restoration.

Conservation NFTs: Digital Assets for Real Impact

Conservation NFTs are unique digital certificates representing our forest efforts, like tree batches or CO₂ offsets.

How we leverage them: In our DApp, verified plantings mint NFTs on Hedera's Token Service. Members stake tokens to own them, turning art or memes into funding sources. Sales support nursery expansions, with royalties reinvested.

Example: A hectare of bamboo becomes an NFT, tradable for carbon credits, with 10% of secondary sales automatically reinvested in Oloolua reforestation.

Hedera Guardian: Transparent Verification

Guardian is an open-source tool for ESG compliance, using dMRV to audit data.

In our conservation: Integrated into our DApp, it verifies plantings with sensors and AI, creating immutable records. This builds trust for donors and ensures accurate reporting for Jaza Miti alignments.

Dovu: Standardizing Carbon Offsets

Dovu, on Hedera, standardizes carbon credits for trading.

How we use it: Our DApp tokenizes offsets via Dovu APIs (e.g., 10 kg CO₂ per tree). This creates "Proof of Carbon" rewards, marketable to companies for ESG needs, generating income for our group.
